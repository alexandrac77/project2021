Crassula ovata: commonly known as jade plant, lucky plant, money plant or money tree.



Jade Plant Information

Growing Jade Plants

Jade Plants grow best in bright light, indoors.

Plant jade in Cactus, Palm & Citrus Potting Mix.

Water your plant when the top 2 inches of soil are dry.

Prune off dead or shriveled branches. You can prune your jade plant into a shape.



TIP: for more jade plants, break off pieces of your plant and stick them in soil so they can form roots. they are easy to propagate.

TIP: blisters on the leaves means the plant is getting too much water.

How to Plant:

- Choose a container with one or more drainage hole(s)

- Fill the container half-way with Potting Mix

- Place the plant in the pot so that top of the root ball is about an inch below the rim of the container, leave room for watering.

- Fill in around the root with more potting mix. pat gently.

- Water your jade plant and let it drain.
